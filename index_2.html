3 Key Native Advertising Campaign Tips - Rise above the Ordinary

Native advertising has become the solution advertisers turn to when they need to produce interesting and fascinating content for users. Proper content, when blended into compelling articles, generates higher user engagement rates and elevates advertiser’s campaign efficiency.

This fact, however, still leaves enough room for questions about native advertising campaigns and their success. Our marketing specialists are ready to answer these questions and have aggregated 3 major tips that should be made use of when launching a native advertising campaign the aim of which is to be effective.

Transparency Levels:

Simply put, make sure your native advertising campaign delivers content that is interesting for a user while attaining and sustaining the user’s trust. Characterizing native ads as sponsored is one way to increase transparency. In general, increasing transparency levels will provide you with more trust from users and boost native ads campaign effectiveness.

Native Ad Blend Mode:

Always keep in mind that your native ads should be displayed as part of a website’s content while being strongly associated with it. They should take user’s needs into account, being customized for the user and his device. This can be achieved by making sure publishers merge your native ads directly into their content flow while implementing the right targeting and campaign optimization techniques.

Goals Coordination:

Your native advertising campaign is bound to earn its success when an advertiser and a publisher work in sync. This leads to native adverts displaying appropriate and suitable content to a user in real-time. This truly comes down to making sure the goals of a publisher are similar to those of an advertiser. Let us say you have a publisher who is into gaming and wants users to buy more games from his website. The right thing to do is to find an advertiser who would motivate users to buy games and make a match.

To summarize, if you want your native advertising campaign bringing you powerful results, make sure you work with the right publisher who displays your ads at the most appropriate place, provides you with transparency and the right targeting and optimization methods.

The next step, after employing these parameters, would be to design the right type of creative – the one that matches both the offer and users’ needs. This will ensure you, as an advertiser, will launch a successful and effective native advertising campaign while connecting you seamlessly with your audiences
